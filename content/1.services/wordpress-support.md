### *5/* Everything worth knowing about WordPress technical support

#title
## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### What is WordPress technical support about?

#description
WordPress technical support includes all activities related to maintenance, update, protection and optimization of the site. It is also ongoing help with technical problems, errors or the need for function expansion.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How much does WordPress administration cost?

#description
The cost of care depends on the project's scope and complexity. Monthly support for standard sites starts from several hundred PLN net, and in the case of extensive services, the price is set individually.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I count on help in case of a site failure?

#description
Yes, we offer both permanent care and ad-hoc WordPress technical assistance in case of failures, errors or problems with the site's operation. We act quickly and effectively.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Do you provide a site backup?

#description
Yes. We configure automatic backups and enable quick site restoration if needed. Backups can be stored on your hosting or externally.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I commission site expansion as part of the care?

#description
Yes. If you have an idea for a new function or need changes - we will implement them as part of additional orders or a plan with development hours.
