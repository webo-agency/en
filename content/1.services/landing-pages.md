### *6/* Everything worth knowing about an effective landing

#title
## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How much does it cost to create a landing page?

#description
The price depends on the project's scope - a simple landing with a contact form costs from approx. 1500-2500 PLN net. An extensive landing page with an individual design, integrations and dynamic elements may cost more. We always prepare an individual quote based on a brief.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Will the landing page be consistent with Google Ads?

#description
Yes. We design **landing pages** consistent with Google Ads, Meta Ads and other advertising platform guidelines. The site will have an appropriate structure, SSL certificate, privacy policy and correct tagging.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How long does it take to create a landing page?

#description
Depending on complexity - from 3 to 10 business days. We are able to implement simple landings very quickly, especially if you have ready-made content. In the case of more extensive projects, we set a schedule individually.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Do you also create landing pages for mailing campaigns or webinars?

#description
Yes. We have experience in designing landing pages for specific types of campaigns: newsletters, event sign-ups, downloading e-books, lead generation campaigns and even employee recruitment. We tailor the layout and content to the campaign goal.
