## *6/* Stages of website implementation

#title
## How does our website implementation process work?

#description
Creating a modern website requires broad knowledge in the field of technology and user-oriented design, as well as a developed process that ensures the fulfillment of assumptions without time delays and within a set budget.
::::
:::

#full

:::block-swiper-tabs-md
---
tabNames:

    - Design
    - Programming
    - Tests and publication
    - Support and development

::::block-swiper-tabs-slide-md
---
icon: '/img/icons/magnifier.svg'

:::::block-header-md
---
maxWidth: 700
---

#title
#### 01. Assumptions analysis

#description
This is the first step we must take in order to implement a website. During the first conversation, we get to know your needs and project assumptions in detail.

**During this stage:**
- We conduct video workshops
- We select the best technology and solutions, optimal for your project
- We prepare an estimate that includes a detailed implementation method and technology stack
- We determine the duration of the project and collection dates<br>

**After accepting the estimate, proposed solutions and deadlines, we sign a cooperation agreement and proceed to implementation.**

:::::
::::


::::block-swiper-tabs-slide-md
---
icon: '/img/icons/www-uxui.svg'
---
:::::block-header-md
---
maxWidth: 700
---

#title
#### 02. UX/UI interface design

#description
During this stage, we work on the look of the website. We start by analyzing the habits of the target group (your customers) and the market environment - all so that users can easily find their way on the new site. Then we develop moodboards (inspiration boards), map out user flows, design UX wireframes, ending with the final look of your site.

**During this stage:**
- You will have insight into every stage of the design work and direct contact with an experienced UX/UI designer.
- You will accept individual stages of your website project implementation - from moodboards to the final look.<br>

**After accepting the final look, the project is ready for the programming stage**

:::::
::::

::::block-swiper-tabs-slide-md
---
icon: '/img/icons/www-code.svg'
---
:::::block-header-md
---
maxWidth: 700
---

#title
#### 03. Programming work

#description
After accepting the interface design, we start work on the comprehensive implementation of the website in the selected technology. We act from configuring the technical infrastructure, through installing required tools to programming the site.

**During this stage:**
- You will have a constant view of the site in the development environment, which will ensure full control over the project
- We will configure the technical infrastructure needed for the efficient operation of the website
- We will program the website along with all required functions and integrations, e.g., GA4.<br>

**After finishing the programming stage, we hand over the website for testing.**

:::::
::::


::::block-swiper-tabs-slide-md
---
icon: '/img/icons/www-tests.svg'
---
:::::block-header-md
---
maxWidth: 700
---

#title
#### 04. Testing and publication

#description
At this stage, we thoroughly check the operation of the website. We perform tests on many devices and screen resolutions (from the smallest smartphones to FHD displays), all to make sure everything works as it should. After passing internal tests, we hand over the site to the client for verification - this is the moment to refine the project and report any comments before publication.

**During this stage:**
- We check the operation of every function, website performance, infrastructure configuration and display of the site on many devices.
- We hand over the site to the client for verification, we perform any corrections to prepare the site for publication.<br>

**After receiving the green light for publication from the client, we launch!**

:::::
::::


::::block-swiper-tabs-slide-md
---
icon: '/img/icons/www-support.svg'
---
:::::block-header-md
---
maxWidth: 700

#title
#### 05. Support and development

#description
Often, website publication is just the beginning, and during the use of the site, ideas for new functionalities, tabs or improvements appear - here we also cannot forget about necessary updates or monitoring performance, security or operational stability.

**As part of the support service, we deal with the following works:**
- Environment (system) update as part of maintenance
- Monitoring website security and speed
- Designing and expanding the website
- Designing and implementing new functionalities and dedicated modules
- Reacting to possible failures<br>

### *8/* Everything you should know before starting to build a website

#title
## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How long does it take to create a website?

#description
Depending on the complexity of the project - from 3 weeks for simple implementations to 8-12 weeks for more advanced projects. We set a detailed schedule after analyzing the assumptions.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How much does it cost to create a website?

#description
The price of a website depends on many factors - the scope of functions, number of subpages, technology, design and integrations (e.g., with external systems). A simple company website can cost from PLN 6,000 net, while an extensive website with headless - several or even several dozen thousand PLN. We quote each project individually after analyzing the needs.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I order only the graphic design of the website?

#description
Of course. We can design the interface (UX/UI) of your website itself - from information architecture to UX wireframes and final design. The project can later be implemented with us or with your developer.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How does your approach differ from working on a ready-made template?

#description
Ready-made templates often limit website development, slow down its operation and generate redundant code. We create websites from scratch - which means full control over every element and a guarantee of performance and security.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Do you offer care and support after website implementation?

#description
Yes. After implementation, we offer technical care, updates and support in expanding the website. You can count on constant cooperation, operational monitoring and functional development as needed.
