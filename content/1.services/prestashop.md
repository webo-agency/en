### *7/* Everything you should know about PrestaShop stores

#title
## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How much does it cost to create a store on PrestaShop?

#description
The cost of **creating a PrestaShop online store** depends on many factors, such as the number of products, required functions, integrations and an individual graphic design. We quote each store individually after analyzing needs and business goals.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How long does it take to create a PrestaShop store?

#description
The implementation time depends on the project's complexity, but standard **PrestaShop store creation** takes from 3 to 8 months. This includes design, coding, testing and technical configurations.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Will PrestaShop work with a large number of products?

#description
Yes, a **PrestaShop store** handles an extensive product catalog very well. Thanks to a well-designed database structure and appropriate configuration, the platform will handle even tens of thousands of products.

:::::
:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I manage the store myself after implementation?

#description
Of course. The PrestaShop administration panel is intuitive and adapted for users without technical knowledge. After store implementation, we provide training and hand over instructions.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### What integrations can I implement in a PrestaShop store?

#description
We can integrate your store with payment systems and couriers, accounting systems, CRM, ERP, as well as with marketing and analytical tools. PrestaShop offers very large possibilities for expansion.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Do you offer support after the store launch?

#description
Yes, we provide full technical support, updates, monitoring of store operation and the possibility of its further expansion. We are a partner that does not disappear after implementation.
