### *8/* Everything you should know before starting and developing a store

#title
## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How much does it cost to create a WooCommerce store?

#description
The price depends on the scope of functionality, number of products, graphic designs and integrations. Simple **WooCommerce online stores** start from several thousand PLN, and extensive systems are quoted individually after analyzing the needs.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### How long does it take to create a WordPress store?

#description
The standard implementation time is 6-12 weeks. Designing, coding, integrations and testing require time for the final product to be refined, fast and reliable.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I manage the store myself after implementation?

#description
Yes. **WooCommerce store creation** assumes simple editing of products, prices, descriptions and orders from an intuitive WordPress panel. We also offer training and documentation.

:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Can I integrate WooCommerce with payments and delivery?

#description
Yes. We integrate stores with payment gateways (e.g., PayU, Przelewy24, Stripe), couriers, invoicing and warehouse systems - we tailor integrations to your business model.
:::::

:::::block-expand-item-md

#title
##### Do you offer technical support after implementation?

#description
Yes, after completing the **WordPress store creation**, we offer technical support, updates, monitoring and the possibility of further store expansion whenever the need arises.
