#description
The result of our work is a visually and **functionally refreshed website**, consistent with the client's visual identity and **contemporary UX/UI design principles**. New subpage views have been integrated with the existing site, which allowed to avoid building the service from scratch. <br><br> Programmed pages have been adapted to display on mobile devices and optimized for speed. The implemented **WordPress component system** combines an attractive visual design with ease of editing, providing solid foundations for further development of the service.
