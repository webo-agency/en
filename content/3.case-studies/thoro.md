#description
We prepared a development environment along with a **project repository on GitHub**, thanks to which we gained full control over the code and the possibility of **safe work on the new layout**. This allowed us to implement changes without affecting the sales continuity of the production store.

#description
The result of our work is a **new look for our client's PrestaShop online store**, which comprehensively refreshed the users' shopping experience. **We programmed over 40 UX/UI views** previously prepared by a branding agency - both in desktop and mobile versions, **working on the existing store infrastructure** (Backend). The project included, among others, implementing the home page, categories, product card, cart and purchasing process, as well as subpages such as "About Us", "Distributors", "Designers" and many others. Thanks to this, the Thoro store gained a **modern and consistent interface**, fully consistent with the brand's new branding, **without changing the operation of the back office** (administrative panel).<br><br> As part of further cooperation, we provide **constant care over the store**, taking care of **operational stability, ongoing support** and developing it with **new functionalities and subpages** according to our client's needs.
