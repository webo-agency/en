::::prose-p3
**Sellineo** is a manufacturer of elements made from large-format ceramic slabs, natural stone, and conglomerates, offering, among others, countertops, washbasins, cladding, stairs, tables, and solutions for interiors and outdoor spaces.

The client approached us with an existing online store that no longer met the needs of a growing brand. The old solution generated technical problems, limited further expansion, and did not allow the quality and aesthetics of the offered products to be properly presented.

The new store was meant to be not only a **stable sales platform**, but also a visually attractive communication tool that showcases the beauty of the materials, highlights their features, and helps users better understand the offer. It was also important to prepare the website for independent management by the client's team, marketing activities, and efficient operation on the German market. The project also had to account for future development of advanced sales formats.

### **UX/UI design of the store**

::::prose-p3
As part of the first stage, we prepared an **extensive UX/UI design of the Sellineo online store**, covering a total of 29 views, including the home page, category pages, product cards, project pages, and subpages presenting the company. One of the main assumptions given by the client was to create a website that would showcase the beauty of the materials and finished products so that visitors could already "feast their eyes" while browsing the offer and naturally feel the quality of the presented solutions.

A key point of the project were the **extensive category and product pages**, which were meant not only to organize the offer, but also to guide the user through the process of learning about the materials, workmanship quality, and personalization options. The content provided by the client served as the foundation for the layout, ensuring educational value while naturally highlighting the most important product information. The design was enriched with photos of products and projects, emphasizing the beauty of the materials and finishes used. Already at the design stage, we also took care of a **modular structure** of the views, preparing blocks that could be reused when **building landing pages** and further subpages.

### **WooCommerce store implementation and data migration**

::::prose-p3
The store was implemented using the **Custom WordPress Development** model, with a full reproduction of the prepared graphic design on the frontend side. We based the implementation on ACF PRO fields, the TailwindCSS framework, Swiper, Docker, and a GIT repository, creating a **flexible and scalable environment** ready for further expansion.

A key element of the implementation was preparing reusable ACF PRO blocks, which allow the client to **independently manage content and build further landing pages** without the need for developer intervention each time. We ensured excellent display of the store on many types of devices (RWD), from desktop computers to tablets and smartphones, as well as high performance and loading speed, especially on the German market. As part of the work, we adapted and **integrated WooCommerce sales functions**, including payments and deliveries, also preparing the store for future sales in other countries and the development of new features. Before the store's publication, we carried out a **migration of over 2,000 products** as well as 500 blog posts, project entries, and Meta data, ensuring a smooth launch of the store and its readiness for further development.

### **Result of the work and further support**

::::prose-p3
The result of our work is a **modern online store** for Sellineo, based on a custom design that showcases the beauty of the products and materials from the very first contact with the brand. The implementation was prepared in such a way that the client can independently manage the website's content and use reusable components when building new landing pages and further subpages. In addition to the migration, we also took care of **optimizing the store's performance** according to PageSpeed guidelines and implementing technical elements according to the SEO agency's recommendations, so that the website is efficient and ready for further growth and building its position in search engines. The final step after the store's publication was **preparing a product feed**, which enables products to be displayed in Google Shopping.

As part of further support, we provide **ongoing development work on the store**, implementing new functionalities, improving existing solutions, helping with the store's day-to-day operation, and preparing the platform for sales in further foreign markets.
