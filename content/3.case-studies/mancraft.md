### **Challenge and areas of activity**

::::prose-p3
The client approached us with a functioning online store made in the PrestaShop 1.6 system. The site had not been updated and adapted to current e-commerce standards for many years. One of the main problems was the lack of proper display of the store on mobile devices and a complicated purchasing process. Additionally, due to the outdated version of PrestaShop, the site had significantly limited development possibilities, also showing deficiencies in the area of performance and security.
::::
:::

:::effect-appear-md
---
class: mx-default tablet:[&_h4]:!mb-6 tablet:[&_.p3_p:not(:last-child)]:!mb-5
---

#### **PrestaShop store migration, interface refresh, development**

::::prose-p3
The first step necessary to fulfill the client's assumptions was the migration of the store from the PrestaShop 1.6 system to PrestaShop 1.7, which enabled **increasing system performance and security** and opening the store to **new development opportunities**. For this purpose, we made a new online store implementing the previously used functionalities and integrations, then moved the database, customer base, products and orders, ending with the implementation of a new responsive template and refreshing the visual layer.

After the migration, we focused on **developing the store with new functionalities** and **optimizing the purchasing process.**
::::
:::

:::effect-appear-md
---
class: mx-default tablet:[&_h4]:!mb-6 tablet:[&_.p3_p:not(:last-child)]:!mb-5
---

#### **Purchasing process optimization**

::::prose-p3
In order to optimize the purchasing process, we **designed and implemented a new product card**, along with a convenient gallery presenting high-resolution photos and fast **PayPal Checkout**. We also introduced changes on the home page, and replaced the standard PrestaShop checkout with the **One Page Checkout** solution, significantly shortening the user path from placing a product in the cart to completing the order.

The form of placing orders by wholesale customers also improved. Wanting to optimize this process, we implemented a **B2B ordering zone**, enabling browsing the entire store offer in the form of a list of products placed on one subpage. Thanks to this functionality, business customers can make a large order with one click "Add to cart", without the need for long-term browsing of product pages or direct contact with a salesperson.

#### **PrestaShop 1.7 Automations**

::::prose-p3
The specificity of Mancraft's activities required the implementation of automation. Creating business customer accounts was **integrated with the VAT VIES system**, which **automatically verifies the correctness of the NIP number and company status**. Stocks and transactions along with payment and delivery data were **integrated with the Baselinker system**. We also implemented **automation of generating and sending a monthly sales report** which speeds up accounting.

In order to increase security, it was necessary to introduce a number of additional security measures, including **server-level certificate automation and encryption of all traffic**, as well as the implementation of additional rules and blockages. The assumption of further development work required the implementation of **CI/CD automation and the GitHub repository**, thanks to which there is full control and standardization of introducing changes in the code.
::::
:::


:::effect-appear-md
---
class: mx-default tablet:[&_h4]:!mb-6 tablet:[&_.p3_p:not(:last-child)]:!mb-5
---

#### **Implementation of a loyalty program for PrestaShop**

::::prose-p3
In the next steps, we implemented a **loyalty program**, thanks to which Mancraft customers can place orders by collecting points for each amount spent. **The transaction value is converted according to the assumed rule**, and points can be exchanged for attractive Mancraft gadgets including hats, scarves, mugs and lanyards. The operation of the loyalty program has been adapted to operate for **many groups of customers with different discount thresholds and store currencies**.
::::

### **Technologies applied**

::::prose-p3
In addition to the PrestaShop 1.7 engine, we adapted many proven solutions for the store implementation. Foreign sales are handled using **Stripe and PayPal gateways**, and shipping is organized by one of the available operators **DHL, Poczta Polska and UPS**. For the Polish market, we implemented the **PayU payment gateway** and shipping to **InPost Parcel Lockers**. Stocks and transactions are integrated with the **Baselinker system**, and transactional emails are handled using the **EmailLabs platform**, which guarantees the highest level of message delivery. To increase security, we used the **Cloudflare** solution, eliminating dangerous traffic and unauthorized access to internal network resources.

### **Result of work and further support**

::::prose-p3
The result of our work is a **multi-currency PrestaShop online store**, supporting payments in CAD, EUR, GBP, PLN, USD currencies and deliveries almost all over the world. Mancraft store customers **can enjoy a new and functional store and numerous functionalities** that make it easier to place orders.

As part of further cooperation, we provide **care over the current operation of the store**, including reacting to failures and introducing required system updates. Our support also includes **optimization work** (including optimizing user experiences and performance), as well as **further development of the store** with new functionalities and subpages.
